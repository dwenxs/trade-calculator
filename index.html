Helpful Notes for Traders

Added to improve usefulness and support AdSense approval.

Why net profit matters more than raw profit

Many traders look at price movement and assume the visible difference between entry and exit is the real result. In practice, fees, funding, and other costs reduce the final number. That is why a proper calculator should always show gross profit, total costs, and net profit separately.

When break-even becomes important

Break-even matters when you are trying to avoid exiting too early or too late. A good break-even value tells you the exact exit level where the position stops being a loss after including the selected costs.

Why multi-target comparison helps

Comparing multiple targets on one screen can save time. Instead of testing one exit after another manually, you can see the cost impact and net return for several target levels at once.

What this calculator does not do

This tool does not connect to exchanges, fetch live prices, or suggest whether a trade is good or bad. It only calculates the financial side of a trade based on the numbers you enter.

Built for direct calculation, not market analysis.
