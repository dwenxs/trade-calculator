Helpful Notes for Traders

Added to improve usefulness and support AdSense approval.

Why net profit matters more than raw profit

Many traders look at visible price movement only. In reality, fees, slippage, funding, and extra costs change the final outcome. That is why a calculator should show gross profit, total costs, and net profit separately.

Why break-even matters

Break-even tells you the exit level where the trade stops being a loss after all selected costs are included. That number is often more useful than looking at the entry price alone.

Multi targets and partial exit are different

Multi-targets compare several possible exit scenarios for the full position. Partial exit actually splits the trade into multiple parts with separate exit targets. Keeping both tools separate makes the results clearer.

What this calculator does not do

This website does not connect to exchanges, fetch live prices, or recommend whether a trade is good or bad. It is a trade math tool only.

Built for direct calculation, not market analysis.
